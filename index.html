About

Tomo Reynolds is a jazz pianist based in Toronto, currently in his third year of the Bachelor of Music program at Humber Polytechnic. He has performed at venues including the Rex Hotel Jazz & Blues Bar.

Available for jazz engagements, private events, and corporate functions, both as a solo pianist and in ensemble settings.
